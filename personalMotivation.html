Inner Republic: Light & Dark

My subconscious long felt like a battleground between the dark and light sides. Like a young Padawan torn between Jedi ideals and the lure of the Sith, I wrestled inwardly with fears, doubts, and the urge to seize control. At the same time, I sensed the light side within me: curiosity, responsibility, and the will to do good through structure and reflection.

This inner conflict echoes the shadows of history—just as the Nazi regime wove seduction and abuse of power into its rise, our minds too can be overtaken by destructive thoughts when left unguided. Yet, much like the Rebels in the galaxy, I faced a choice: either succumb to dark temptation or strategically leverage my strengths and work on my weaknesses.

Today, my motivation is to consciously maintain that balance: to turn the dark side of my doubts into productive mindfulness, and to translate the light side of my visions into concrete action. In doing so, I anchor my decisions in ethics and foresight—and greet each new day as the guardian of my own inner Republic.
